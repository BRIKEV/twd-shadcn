How do you test a shadcn/ui Accordion component?

Here's what I check:
- Does it render with the correct button roles?
- Is the first item expanded by default (aria-expanded)?
- Does clicking expand another item?
- Does only one stay open at a time?

These are simple questions but getting accessibility right matters. Swipe through to see the test code and how it works in the real browser.
