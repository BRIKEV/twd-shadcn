Testing a shadcn/ui Accordion is more than checking if it opens and closes.

You want to make sure:
→ The correct ARIA roles are in place (button, aria-expanded)
→ The first panel is expanded by default
→ Clicking a header expands its content
→ Only one panel stays open at a time

Swipe through to see how I test this with real browser interactions — no jsdom, no guesswork.
