How do you test a shadcn/ui Select component?

Here's what I check:
- Does it render with the correct combobox role?
- Does clicking open the dropdown?
- Can you query portal-rendered options? (hint: screenDomGlobal)
- Does the selected value update correctly?

The tricky part with Select is that options render in a portal — outside the component tree. Swipe through to see how to handle that in your tests.
